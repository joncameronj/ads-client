Scale a campaign by adjusting all ad set budgets by a percentage. Respects Meta's 20% rule for safe scaling.

Use the `mcp__meta-ads__scale_campaign` tool.

Required from user:
- campaign_id: Campaign ID to scale
- scale_pct: Percentage to change budgets (+20 = 20% increase, -10 = 10% decrease). Max +100%, min -50%.

Confirm the scaling and show the new budget amounts.
