Update the daily or lifetime budget for an ad set or campaign. Amounts in dollars.

Use the `mcp__meta-ads__manage_budget` tool.

Required from user:
- object_id: Ad set or campaign ID
- level: "adset" or "campaign"

At least one of:
- daily_budget: New daily budget in dollars
- lifetime_budget: New lifetime budget in dollars

Confirm the budget change and show the new amount.
